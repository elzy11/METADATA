Metadata Metaphors is a collection of three data visualizations of how Meta extracts profit from human attention — built as part of a bachelor's thesis on data capitalism and surveillance at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.

You are about to move through three pieces: a live trading terminal, a growing tree, and a galaxy you are already inside. Each one shows the same system from a different distance — the evidence, the mechanism, the condition.

Stock prices, revenue figures, and user counts are drawn from Meta's audited public filings.
